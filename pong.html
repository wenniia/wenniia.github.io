Arduino Pong

Inspired by the popular 1972 Pong arcade video game, this project will be taking inspiration from mechanics of the Pong game with an Arduino-powered twist. The game will utilize a color OLED display to screen the graphics, illustrating the scoreboard, paddles, and ball. The game will be shown on a color OLED display, showing the scoreboard, paddles, and ball. Through the 2-player function, this game will give the option to players of playing a classic game solo or with other people. Additionally, players can play the game with color. Whether this game is a first or not for the player, this timeless classic will be fun alone or with other people.
