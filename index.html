Annie Wang is an incoming second-year studying electrical engineering at UCSB. Doubling as a self-taught digital artist, she enjoys using this illustrative medium as a means of expressing her interests and emotions. With a profound interest in expressing art reflecting the intricacies of light, she examines how properties of light distort and carve form, altering and complicating how people understand and verify their perceptions of the real world.
